# Factor Dictionary

## Scale
- 0 = absent / no evidence
- 1 = low
- 2 = moderate
- 3 = high
- 9 = unknown / insufficient evidence

## Political
- political_fragmentation: degree of breakdown of centralized authority into regional or competing power centers
- elite_conflict: intensity of conflict among ruling elites
- succession_crisis: instability in transfer of power
- legitimacy_crisis: loss of belief in the right to rule
- administrative_overload: inability of institutions to manage complexity
- corruption_governance_failure: systemic governance weakness or predation

## Social
- social_inequality: concentration of wealth/power causing strain
- social_unrest_rebellion: revolts, riots, organized internal unrest
- demographic_pressure: population strain relative to carrying capacity
- migration_pressure: destabilizing inward or outward movement of people
- ethnic_sectarian_fragmentation: internal identity-based division
- urban_decline: decline of cities and urban systems

## Economic
- fiscal_crisis: state revenue and budgetary breakdown
- taxation_extraction_pressure: coercive or unsustainable extraction burden
- trade_disruption: breakdown of trade routes or exchange systems
- inflation_currency_instability: monetary instability, debasement, inflation
- resource_dependency: dependence on narrow external or internal resource base
- agricultural_decline: declining farm productivity or cultivated output

## Environmental
- drought_climate_stress: drought or aridity stress
- flood_environmental_shock: flooding or hydrological disruption
- temperature_anomaly: meaningful cooling or warming affecting stability
- ecological_degradation: erosion, salinization, deforestation, depletion
- food_insecurity: shortages, famine risk, supply instability

## Military / geopolitical
- external_invasion_pressure: pressure from external enemies
- civil_war_internal_conflict: organized internal armed conflict
- military_overstretch: commitments exceed sustainable capacity
- territorial_loss: loss of provinces, regions, strategic control

## Resilience
- institutional_rigidity: inability to reform under stress
- adaptive_capacity: ability to respond and reorganize
- logistics_food_storage_resilience: transport, storage, logistics redundancy
- alliance_network_strength: strength of supportive alliance network
- recovery_capacity: ability to recover after major shocks
